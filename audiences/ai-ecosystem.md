I wanted to make sure you know about a programme I think could use your help — in a few specific ways.

Tangent is a 24-month programme providing up to S$200,000 per participant for young people in Singapore (18–21, or 18–22 post-NS) who want to build something real. It's focused on people who wouldn't show up on the radar of the usual incubators and accelerators — capable and motivated, but not yet legible to conventional programmes.

It starts with a four-week programme: S$1,000, AI tools, and human guides. No mandatory curriculum. Those who want to continue at the end of four weeks pitch for the full 24-month programme.

Three things that would make a real difference:
- Refer a young person who belongs in this programme
- Help with AI tool access — subscriptions or API credits for participants
- Come in as a guide during a cohort to talk about AI business models or deployment

https://tangent.org.sg/ has details on the programme and how it works, how to apply, how to refer someone, and how to get involved as a guide.
