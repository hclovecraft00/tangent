I wanted to share something I'm building and ask for your help with a few things.

Tangent is a 24-month fellowship providing up to S$200,000 for young people in Singapore (18–21, or 18–22 post-NS) who want to build something real. We're focused on people who wouldn't show up on the radar of Antler or NUS Enterprise — capable and motivated, but not yet legible to conventional programmes.

The fellowship begins with a four-week programme: S$1,000, AI tools, and human guides. No mandatory curriculum. Those who want to continue at the end of four weeks pitch for the full 24-month fellowship.

Three things I'd like to ask:
- Refer a young person who belongs in this programme
- Help with AI tool access — subscriptions or API credits for participants
- Come in as a guide during a cohort to talk about AI business models or deployment

tangent.org.sg has details on the programme and how it works, how to apply, how to refer someone, and how to get involved as a guide.
